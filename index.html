Student Check-In

Step 1: scan the desk QR code. Step 2: place the phone on the desk sensor area. Step 3: remain seated during class.

How this works

The QR code identifies the student and desk. The phone sensor area represents the real desk spot where the phone is placed during class. The seat sensor confirms the student is physically present. Emergency phone removal is allowed for a limited time only.

Focus and Phone Control Features

Phone-Free Class Time

The system encourages students to keep their phones on the desk sensor during class, helping reduce unnecessary checking of notifications and social media.

Emergency Use Limit

Students may remove their phones for emergencies, but the timed return rule helps prevent long periods of phone use during lectures.

Focus Score Tracking

Students receive a focus score based on consistent attendance, staying seated, and limited phone removal, helping them monitor classroom discipline.

Progress Awareness

Attendance history, streaks, and alerts make students more aware of their habits and can motivate them to reduce phone dependence over time.
