Smart Chair System

Prototype system for reducing phone distraction and automating attendance in classrooms.

Smart Chair Login

This is the first screen of the system. Users log in according to their role. Students access check-in and attendance tracking, while professors access attendance analytics and records.

Students will be directed to the check-in system first, then they can view their attendance performance. Professors will be directed to the dashboard for attendance monitoring and analytics.

Welcome

Student Check-In

Step 1: Scan the QR code on your desk. Step 2: Place your phone on the desk sensor area. Step 3: Stay seated during class. This sequence helps the system record attendance more accurately and reduce phone distraction.

How the system works:

The QR code identifies the student and desk. The phone sensor area represents the real desk sensor where the phone should be placed during class. The seat sensor confirms that the student remains physically present. If the phone is removed for too long or the seat stays empty, the student may be marked absent.
